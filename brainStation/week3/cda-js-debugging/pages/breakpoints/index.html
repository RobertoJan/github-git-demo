Some Terminology

Execution
synonymous with "run". It's used to indicate when a program is run by a computer.
Breakpoint
also known as a pause, itâ€™s a marker on a program to stop or pause execution intentionally and let you explore the environment at that point in execution.
Stepping
execution takes place statement by statement. Stepping is the act of going through execution statement by statement, pausing at each one.
